Here’s a professional and concise email template you can use to schedule a follow-up call for your EHR integration project. Adjust the details (e.g., time zones, names, and specifics) as needed:

---
**Subject:** Follow-Up Call to Discuss [EHR Integration Project Name] – [Proposed Date/Time]

Dear [Recipient's Name],

Thank you for your time and insights during our recent discussion about the [EHR Integration Project Name]. I’ve reviewed the key points and would love to schedule a follow-up call next week to align on next steps, address any questions, and outline a preliminary plan.

**Proposed Options:**
1. **[Date] at [Time, e.g., 10:00 AM EST]** – [Duration, e.g., 30 minutes]
2. **[Date] at [Time, e.g., 2:00 PM PST]** – [Duration]
3. **[Alternative Date/Time]** – If these times don’t work, please let me know your availability.

**Agenda Highlights:**
- Review [specific topic, e.g., "data mapping requirements" or "go-live timeline"]
- Clarify [any open questions, e.g., "vendor compatibility" or "budget considerations"]
- Next steps for [e.g., "proof of concept" or "contract signing"]

Please let me know which time works best for you or suggest alternatives. I’m happy to accommodate your schedule. Looking forward to your confirmation!

Best regards,
[Your Full Name]
[Your Job Title]
[Your Company Name]
[Your Contact Information]
[Optional: Calendar Link (e.g., Calendly or Outlook invite)]

---
### **Tips to Customize:**
1. **Tone:** Adjust formality based on your relationship (e.g., more casual if you’re close).
2. **Agenda:** Include 2–3 bullet points to show you’ve prepared.
3. **Flexibility:** Offer 2–3 time slots to make it easy for them to respond.
4. **Urgency:** If time-sensitive, add: *"Given the [deadline/phase], I’d appreciate confirming by [date] to finalize the plan."*
5. **Attachments:** If relevant, link to docs (e.g., "See attached for reference materials").
